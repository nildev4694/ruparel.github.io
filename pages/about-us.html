New medicines are rigorously tested at our hospital for efficacy, and clinical findings are shared with laboratory researchers, resulting in improvements in patient care. The foundation of our comprehensive ENT facility is translational research, which allows us to precisely treat a wide spectrum of ENT diseases. In addition, our clinic offers the most up-to-date minimally invasive techniques in state-of-the-art surgical suites, resulting in speedier recovery, fewer infection rates, and less discomfort following the procedure. The architectural features of the center, such as floor-to-ceiling windows, art exhibits, and a fifth-floor outdoor terrace with views of Coral Gables, create a light-filled environment that promotes patient rehabilitation and well-being.
